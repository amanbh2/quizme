# 🧪 General Science & Technology Study Notes (BPSC CCE)

General Science is a massive pillar of the BPSC CCE Prelims, consistently contributing **30 questions** (20% of the entire exam). Biology has the highest weightage, followed by Physics and Chemistry.

---

## 📂 Sub-Subject Structure

1. **`biology/`** (Highest Weightage)
   - *Human Physiology*: Digestive, circulatory, nervous, respiratory, and endocrine systems. Hormones and enzymes.
   - *Cell Biology*: Plant cell vs. animal cell, cell organelles (mitochondria, ribosomes, plastids, cell wall), mitosis vs. meiosis.
   - *Diseases & Nutrition*: Vitamins and deficiency diseases, bacterial/viral/fungal diseases (symptoms, vectors), proteins, carbohydrates, fats.
   - *Plant Physiology*: Photosynthesis, plant hormones (auxin, gibberellin, cytokinin, abscisic acid, ethylene), transpiration, plant kingdom classification.
2. **`physics/`**
   - *Mechanics*: Units and measurements (SI units), motion, speed, velocity, force, Newton's laws, work, energy, power, gravity, friction.
   - *Light & Sound*: Reflection, refraction, dispersion, total internal reflection, mirrors & lenses (defects of vision like myopia/hyperopia and their corrective lenses), wave properties, Doppler effect, speed of sound in different media.
   - *Heat & Electricity*: Temperature scales, conduction, convection, radiation, Ohm's law, resistance in series/parallel, heating effect, domestic electrical wiring.
3. **`chemistry/`**
   - *Matter*: States of matter, elements, compounds, mixtures, physical vs. chemical changes.
   - *Atomic Structure*: Protons, neutrons, electrons, isotopes, isobars, chemical bonding.
   - *Acids, Bases & Salts*: pH scale, indicators, properties, neutralization, buffer solutions.
   - *Metals & Non-metals*: Ores and metallurgy (e.g., brass, bronze, steel alloys), rusting of iron, properties of hydrogen, oxygen, nitrogen, carbon allotropes (diamond, graphite, fullerene).
   - *Everyday Chemistry*: Soaps/detergents, polymers (polythene, bakelite, nylon), glass, cement, explosives (TNT, RDX), fertilizers.
4. **`technology/`**
   - *Space Technology*: ISRO missions (Chandrayaan, Gaganyaan, Aditya-L1), launch vehicles (PSLV, GSLV, LVM3), types of orbits.
   - *Defense & Biotech*: Indian missile defense systems (Agni, Prithvi, BrahMos), nuclear program, GM crops (Bt Cotton, Bt Brinjal), CRISPR, cloning.
   - *Emerging Tech*: AI, Nanotech, Semiconductors, Quantum computing basics.

---

## 🎯 BPSC Preparation Strategy

- **Vitamin Table**: Create a detailed chart of Vitamin chemical names (e.g., A = Retinol, B1 = Thiamine, C = Ascorbic Acid) and their deficiency diseases. BPSC almost always asks a question on this.
- **Scientific Units**: Memorize the SI units of physical quantities (light year, parsec, sound decibels, frequency Hertz, power of lens Dioptre).
- **Alloys & Compositions**: Know the constituent metals of common alloys (Brass = Cu+Zn, Bronze = Cu+Sn, German Silver = Cu+Zn+Ni, Solder = Pb+Sn).
- **Lenses**: Memorize which lenses correct Myopia (concave) and Hyperopia (convex).
