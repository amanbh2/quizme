# 📰 Current Affairs Study Notes (BPSC CCE)

Current Affairs constitutes **25 to 30 questions** in the BPSC CCE Prelims. The questions are highly factual, focusing on ranks, names of personalities, summits, awards, sports, and dates.

---

## 📂 Sub-Subject Structure

1. **`national-affairs/`**
   - *Government Initiatives*: New policies, portals, apps launched by ministries.
   - *Appointments*: Chiefs of defense, judiciary, commissions (CAG, CEC, Attorney General, RBI Governor), new heads of institutions.
   - *Summits & Reports*: G20, BRICS, SCO summits (venues, themes); India's ranks in global indices (Happiness Index, Press Freedom, Gender Gap).
2. **`international-affairs/`**
   - *Global Events*: Elections of new Presidents/PMs of major countries, bilateral exercises (military/navy/air force), major international treaties, conflicts.
3. **`bihar-current-events/`** (Very High Weightage)
   - *State Schemes*: Har Ghar Gangajal, Saat Nischay Part-2, district-wise developments.
   - *Appointments & Achievements*: Governor/Chief Justice of Patna High Court, awards won by people from Bihar (Padma awards, Sahitya Akademi).
   - *Bihar Indices*: Performance of Bihar in national indices (NITI Aayog SDG Index, Export Preparedness Index).
4. **`miscellaneous/`**
   - *Awards*: Nobel Prizes, Bharat Ratna, National Film Awards, Oscar winners.
   - *Sports*: Olympics/Paralympics, ICC World Cups, Grand Slams, Asian Games (winners and India's ranks/medals).
   - *Books & Authors*: Notable books released in the past year.

---

## 🎯 BPSC Preparation Strategy

- **Saat Nischay Part-2**: Memorize the exact seven resolutions of Bihar's Saat Nischay Part-2 scheme. BPSC often asks "which of the following is NOT part of Saat Nischay".
- **Military Exercises**: Create a quick list of bilateral exercises involving India (e.g., Nomad Elephant, Samudra Shakti, Surya Kiran).
- **Index Ranks**: Memorize both the rank of India and the organization publishing the index (e.g., World Economic Forum, Transparency International).
