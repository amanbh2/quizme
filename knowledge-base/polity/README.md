# ⚖️ Indian Polity & Governance Study Notes (BPSC CCE)

Polity accounts for **10 to 15 questions** in BPSC CCE. Questions are generally straightforward, factual, and article-oriented, making this a high-scoring section if you memorize key articles and amendments.

---

## 📂 Sub-Subject Structure

1. **`constitution-core/`**
   - *Historical Background*: Regulating Acts, Government of India Acts (1919, 1935), making of the Constitution, sources of the Constitution.
   - *Preamble, Parts, & Schedules*: Memorize the 12 Schedules and the core vocabulary of the Preamble.
   - *Fundamental Rights, DPSPs, & Fundamental Duties*: Articles 12 to 51A (extremely high yield).
   - *Constitutional Amendments*: 42nd, 44th, 73rd, 74th, 86th, 101st (GST) onwards.
2. **`union-government/`**
   - *Executive*: President (powers, impeachment, pardon, veto), Vice-President, Prime Minister, Council of Ministers, Attorney General.
   - *Legislature*: Parliament (Lok Sabha & Rajya Sabha structure, legislative procedures, money bills, joint sittings, parliamentary committees).
   - *Judiciary*: Supreme Court (jurisdiction, judicial review, writs, judicial activism, PIL).
3. **`state-government/`**
   - *Executive & Legislature*: Governor (discretionary powers, ordinance making), Chief Minister, State Legislative Assembly & Council.
   - *Local Self-Government*: 73rd & 74th Amendment Acts, Panchayati Raj Institutions (PRIs), Municipalities.
4. **`bodies-commissions/`**
   - *Constitutional Bodies*: Election Commission, UPSC, SPSC (BPSC), Finance Commission, CAG, National Commissions for SC/ST/OBC.
   - *Non-Constitutional Bodies*: NITI Aayog, NHRC, Central Information Commission (CIC), Lokpal.

---

## 🎯 BPSC Preparation Strategy

- **Article & Schedule Memorization**: Memorize key articles (e.g., Article 72, 110, 123, 143, 280, 312, 324, 356, 368). BPSC regularly asks direct matching questions for articles.
- **Panchayati Raj**: Since Bihar was the first state to offer 50% reservation to women in panchayats (2006), BPSC tests panchayat articles (Article 243 to 243O) very thoroughly.
- **Writs**: Understand the differences between *Habeas Corpus*, *Mandamus*, *Prohibition*, *Certiorari*, and *Quo-Warranto*.
