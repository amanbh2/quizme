This note contains fact-checked, high-yield data from the **Bihar Budget 2026-27**, presented in the Bihar Legislative Assembly by State Finance Minister **Bijendra Prasad Yadav** on **February 3, 2026**.

## 📅 Background & Overview

*   **Presented On:** February 3, 2026.
*   **Presented By:** Bijendra Prasad Yadav (Finance Minister of Bihar).

## 🏛️ Departmental Outlays & Priorities

### 1. Education Department (Highest Total Outlay)
*   **Total Allocation:** **₹60,204.61 Crore** (**17.32%** of the total state budget).
*   **National Comparison:** Bihar allocates **21.7%** of its overall developmental expenditure towards education — significantly higher than the average for Indian states.

### 2. Rural Development Department (Highest Scheme Outlay)
*   **Scheme Outlay Allocation:** **₹22,392.47 Crore**.
*   **Analysis:** Received the highest scheme outlay to target rural housing, employment, and roads, aligning with Bihar's rural-heavy demographics (~90% rural population).

### 3. FRBM Limits & Financial Discipline
*   **FRBM Target:** The Bihar Fiscal Responsibility and Budget Management (FRBM) Act caps the fiscal deficit at **3% of GSDP**.
*   **Deficit Path:** Bihar brought its fiscal deficit down to **2.99%** for 2026-27, recovering from a high deficit of 4.2% in FY 2024-25.
