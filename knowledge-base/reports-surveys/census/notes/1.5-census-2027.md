# 👥 Topic 1.5: Census 2027 & Developments — Census Study Notes

This note outlines the features, transitions, and timelines of the upcoming 16th Indian Census.

---

## 📅 Chronology & Rescheduling

*   **Original Schedule:** Due in **2021** as the 16th Census of India.
*   **Postponement:** Delayed indefinitely due to the **COVID-19 pandemic**.
*   **Resumed Operations:** Officially designated as **Census 2027** and is currently actively underway.

---

## ⚡ Key Methodological Transitions

*   **First Digital Census:**
    *   Transition from traditional paper schedules to collection via **mobile applications** and web-based portals.
    *   Eliminates paper forms to reduce compilation lag and increase accuracy.
*   **Self-Enumeration:**
    *   Citizens have the option to log in to the official portal (**se.census.gov.in**) and input their demographic details online.
    *   Generates a **Self-Enumeration ID (SE ID)**, which is then shared with the field enumerator during verification visits.
*   **Caste-Based Counting:**
    *   The Census 2027 includes a comprehensive caste-based enumeration.
    *   This is the **first time all castes are counted in a national census since 1931**.

---

## ⚙️ Operational Phases & Timelines

The census is conducted in two distinct phases:

| Phase | Operation Title | Active Timeline | Scope & Activities |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Phase I** | Houselisting & Housing Census (HLO) | **April 1, 2026, to September 30, 2026** | Structural listings, housing conditions, and utility/asset ownership records. |
| **Phase II**| Population Enumeration (PE) | **February 2027** (Revision: late Feb to March 1, 2027) | Personal, social, economic, educational, and demographic metrics collection. |

> [!NOTE]
> Customized timelines are scheduled for snow-bound, non-synchronous areas of Ladakh, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, and Jammu & Kashmir.
