## 🏆 District-Wise Superlatives & Rankings

### 1. Population & Area
*   **Most Populous:** (1) **Patna** (58.38 Lakh - only district >50 Lakh), (2) East Champaran (50.99 Lakh).
*   **Least Populous:** (1) **Sheikhpura** (6.36 Lakh), (2) Sheohar (6.56 Lakh).
*   **Largest Area:** **West Champaran** (5,228 sq. km) \| **Smallest Area:** **Sheohar** (349 sq. km).

### 2. Population Density
*   **Highest Density:** (1) **Sheohar** (**1,880** persons/sq. km - smallest area causes highest density), (2) Patna (1,823).
*   **Lowest Density:** (1) **Kaimur (Bhabua)** (**488** persons/sq. km - low density due to hills/forests).

### 3. Decadal Growth Rate (2001–2011)
*   **Highest Growth:** (1) **Madhepura** (**31.12%**), (2) Kishanganj (30.40%).
*   **Lowest Growth:** (1) **Gopalganj** (**18.83%**), (2) Darbhanga (19.47%).

### 4. Sex Ratio (Overall & Child)
*   **Highest Sex Ratio (Overall):** **Gopalganj** (**1,021** - only district in Bihar where females outnumber males).
*   **Lowest Sex Ratio (Overall):** **Munger** (**879**), followed by Bhagalpur (880).
*   **Highest Child Sex Ratio (0–6):** **Kishanganj** (**971**).
*   **Lowest Child Sex Ratio (0–6):** **Vaishali / Patna** (**904**).

### 5. Literacy Rate (Total & Gender-wise)
*   **Highest Literacy:** **Rohtas** (**73.37%** - ranks **1st** in total, male [82.88%], and female [62.97%] literacy).
*   **Lowest Literacy:** **Purnia** (**51.08%** - lowest overall and lowest male [59.06%] literacy).
*   **Lowest Female Literacy:** **Saharsa** (**41.68%**).

### 6. Urbanization Level
*   **Highest Urbanized:** **Patna** (**43.07%**) \| **Lowest Urbanized:** **Samastipur** (**3.47%**).

## 💼 Caste & Religious Demographics in Bihar

*   **SC Population:** Gaya has the highest SC percentage (**30.4%**) \| Kishanganj has the lowest (**6.7%**).
*   **ST Population:** West Champaran has the highest ST percentage (**6.35%**) \| Sheohar has the lowest (**0.01%**).
*   **Religious Share:** Hindu: **82.7%** \| Muslim: **16.9%** (higher than national average of 14.2%) \| Christian: **0.12%** \| Sikh: **0.02%**.
*   **Muslim Concentration:** **Kishanganj** is the highest at **~68%**, followed by Katihar (~44.5%) and Araria (~42.9%).

## 🗣️ Language & Official Status

*   **Official Languages:** **Hindi** (Primary) \| **Maithili** (Second Official - the only Bihar language in the **8th Schedule** of the Constitution, added via the 92nd Amendment in 2003).
*   **Regional Dialects:** Bhojpuri (West Bihar), Magahi (South Bihar), Angika (Southeast), Bajjika (North-Central).
