## 🏆 State & UT Superlatives (Top and Bottom Ranks)

### 1. Population
*   **Most Populous States:** (1) Uttar Pradesh (19.98 Cr), (2) Maharashtra (11.24 Cr), (3) **Bihar (10.41 Cr)**, (4) West Bengal (9.13 Cr).
*   **Least Populous States:** (1) Sikkim (6.10 Lakh), (2) Mizoram (10.97 Lakh), (3) Arunachal Pradesh (13.84 Lakh).
*   **UT Extremes:** Most Populous: **Delhi** (1.67 Cr) \| Least Populous: **Lakshadweep** (64.4k).
*   **District Extremes:** Most Populous: **Thane** (MH, 1.10 Cr) \| Least Populous: **Dibang Valley** (AR, 8,004).

### 2. Population Density (persons/sq. km)
*   **Highest Density States:** (1) **Bihar (1,106)**, (2) West Bengal (1,028), (3) Kerala (860).
*   **Lowest Density States:** (1) Arunachal Pradesh (17), (2) Mizoram (52), (3) Sikkim (86).
*   **UT Extremes:** Highest: **Delhi** (11,320) \| Lowest: **Andaman & Nicobar** (46).

### 3. Sex Ratio (females / 1000 males)
*   **Highest Sex Ratio States:** (1) **Kerala (1,084)**, (2) Tamil Nadu (996), (3) Andhra Pradesh (993).
*   **Lowest Sex Ratio States:** (1) **Haryana (879)**, (2) Jammu & Kashmir (889), (3) Sikkim (890).
*   **UT Extremes:** Highest: **Puducherry** (1,037) \| Lowest: **Daman & Diu** (618).
*   **Child Sex Ratio (0-6) Ranks:** Highest: **Arunachal Pradesh (972)** \| Lowest: **Haryana (834)**.

### 4. Literacy Rate
*   **Highest Literacy States:** (1) **Kerala (94.00%)**, (2) Mizoram (91.30%), (3) Tripura (87.75%).
*   **Lowest Literacy States:** (1) **Bihar (61.80%)**, (2) Arunachal Pradesh (65.38%), (3) Rajasthan (66.11%).
*   **UT Extremes:** Highest: **Lakshadweep** (91.85%) \| Lowest: **Dadra & Nagar Haveli** (76.24%).

### 5. Decadal Growth Rate (2001–2011)
*   **Highest Growth States:** (1) **Meghalaya (27.9%)**, (2) Arunachal Pradesh (26.0%), (3) **Bihar (25.42%)**.
*   **Lowest Growth States:** (1) **Nagaland (-0.6%)** (*Only state with negative growth*), (2) Kerala (4.9%).

### 6. Urbanization Level
*   **Most Urbanized States:** Goa (62.2%), Mizoram (52.1%), Tamil Nadu (48.4%).
*   **Least Urbanized States:** Himachal Pradesh (10.0%), **Bihar (11.3%)**, Assam (14.1%).

## 💼 Caste, Work & Religion Demographics

### 1. Scheduled Castes (SC)
*   **National Percentage:** **16.6%** (20.14 Cr).
*   **Highest Proportion State:** **Punjab** (31.9% of state population).
*   **Largest Absolute Population:** Uttar Pradesh.

### 2. Scheduled Tribes (ST)
*   **National Percentage:** **8.6%** (10.43 Cr).
*   **Highest Proportion State:** **Mizoram** (94.4% of state population).
*   **Largest Absolute Population:** Madhya Pradesh.
*   > [!NOTE]
    > Punjab, Haryana, Delhi, Chandigarh, and Puducherry have **no notified ST populations**.

### 3. Work force Shift
*   **Work Participation Rate:** **39.8%** of total population (Main: 29.9%, Marginal: 9.9%).
*   **Structural Trend:** Cultivators fell to **24.6%** of workforce, while **Agricultural Labourers rose to 30.0%** (indicates rising rural landlessness).

### 4. National Religious Demographics
*   **Breakdown:** Hindu: **79.8%** \| Muslim: **14.2%** \| Christian: **2.3%** \| Sikh: **1.7%** \| Buddhist: **0.7%** \| Jain: **0.4%** \| Others/Not Stated: **0.9%**.
