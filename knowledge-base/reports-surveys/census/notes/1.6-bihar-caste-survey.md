# 👥 Topic 1.6: Bihar Caste-Based Survey 2023 — Census Study Notes

This note compiles the high-yield statistical findings of the **Bihar Caste-Based Survey (Bihar Jaati Adharit Ganana) 2023**, released on **October 2, 2023**.

---

## 📅 Survey Overview & Population

*   **Total Population of Bihar (2023):** **13,07,25,310 (13.07 Cr)** (*up from 10.41 Cr in 2011*).
*   **Gender Split:** Male: 6,41,30,537 \| Female: 6,11,38,460.
*   **Residents Outside the State:** 53,72,022.

---

## 📊 Social Category Demographics Table (2023)

| Social Category | Population | Percentage Share in Bihar | Key BPSC Insights |
| :--- | :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Extremely Backward Classes (EBC)** | 4,70,80,514 | **36.01%** | **Largest social group** in the state. |
| **Backward Classes (BC)** | 3,54,63,936 | **27.13%** | Second largest social category. |
| **Scheduled Castes (SC)** | 2,56,89,820 | **19.65%** | Increased from **15.70%** in 2011. |
| **General Category (Unreserved)** | 2,02,91,679 | **15.52%** | Includes all upper-caste groups. |
| **Scheduled Tribes (ST)** | 21,99,361 | **1.68%** | Marginal increase from **1.28%** in 2011. |

## 🕌 Religious Demographics Shift (2023 vs. 2011)

*   **Hindu:** **81.99%** (*down from 82.70% in 2011*).
*   **Muslim:** **17.70%** (*up from 16.90% in 2011*).
*   **Minorities:** Buddhist (**0.08%**) \| Christian (**0.05%**) \| Sikh (**0.01%**) \| Jain (**0.009%**) \| No Religion (**0.12%**).
