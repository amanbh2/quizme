# 👥 Census Updates — Census 2027 & Bihar Caste Survey 2023

This note compiles the latest updates on the upcoming census of India and the landmark state-level surveys relevant to BPSC.

---

## 📅 Census 2027 (16th Indian Census)

Originally scheduled for 2021, the decadal census of India was postponed indefinitely due to the COVID-19 pandemic. It has officially resumed operations as **Census 2027** and is being conducted in two major phases.

### 1. Key Highlights & Methodology
*   **First Digital Census:** For the first time in Indian history, census data is being collected digitally via mobile applications and web portals, eliminating paper forms.
*   **Self-Enumeration:** Citizens have the option to self-enumerate online via the official portal (**se.census.gov.in**) prior to the enumerator's physical visit. On completion, a **Self-Enumeration ID (SE ID)** is generated to be shared with the field enumerator.
*   **Caste Enumeration:** The Census 2027 incorporates a full-scale caste-based enumeration, marking the first time all castes are counted in a national census since the **1931 Census**.

### 2. Operational Phases
*   **Phase I: Houselisting & Housing Census (HLO):**
    *   **Timeline:** Active from **April 1, 2026, to September 30, 2026**.
    *   **Activities:** Enumerators are visiting houses to list structures, record housing conditions, and catalog household assets/amenities.
*   **Phase II: Population Enumeration (PE):**
    *   **Timeline:** Scheduled for **February 2027** (with the official revision round ending on March 1, 2027).
    *   **Activities:** Enumeration of all individuals, collecting detailed socioeconomic, educational, and demographic parameters.
    *   **Non-Synchronous Areas:** Snow-bound regions of Ladakh, Himachal Pradesh, Uttarakhand, and Jammu & Kashmir have customized timelines.

## 📊 Bihar Caste-Based Survey 2023

Due to delays in the national census, the Government of Bihar conducted its own **Caste-Based Survey (Bihar Jaati Adharit Ganana)** in two phases during 2023. The final report was released on **October 2, 2023**, and is highly tested in recent BPSC exams.

### 1. General Findings
*   **Bihar's Total Population (2023):** **13,07,25,310 (13.07 Crore)**, up from 10.41 Crore in 2011.
*   **Gender Split:** Male: 6,41,30,537; Female: 6,11,38,460.
*   **Bihar Residents Outside State:** 53,72,022.

### 2. Social Category Demographics

| Social Category | Population | percentage Share in Bihar |
| :--- | :--- | :--- |
| **Extremely Backward Classes (EBC)** | 4,70,80,514 | **36.01%** (Largest group) |
| **Backward Classes (BC)** | 3,54,63,936 | **27.13%** |
| **Scheduled Castes (SC)** | 2,56,89,820 | **19.65%** (Increased from 15.7% in 2011) |
| **General Category (Unreserved)** | 2,02,91,679 | **15.52%** |
| **Scheduled Tribes (ST)** | 21,99,361 | **1.68%** |

### 3. Major Caste Populations (Top Groups)
*   **Yadav:** 1.86 Crore (**14.26%** of total population — largest individual caste).
*   **Ravidas (Chamar):** 68.6 Lakh (5.25%).
*   **Dusadh (Paswan):** 69.4 Lakh (5.31%).
*   **Koiri (Kushwaha):** 55.0 Lakh (4.21%).
*   **Kurmi:** 37.6 Lakh (2.87%).
*   **Brahman:** 47.8 Lakh (3.66%).
*   **Rajput:** 45.1 Lakh (3.45%).
*   **Bhumihar:** 37.8 Lakh (2.89%).

### 4. Religious Demographics (Bihar 2023)
*   **Hindu:** **81.99%**
*   **Muslim:** **17.70%** (Increased from 16.9% in 2011)
*   **Christian:** 0.05%
*   **Sikh:** 0.01%
*   **Buddhist:** 0.08%
