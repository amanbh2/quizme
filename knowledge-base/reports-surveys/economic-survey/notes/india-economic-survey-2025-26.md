# 📈 India Economic Survey 2025-26 — Complete BPSC Study Notes

This note contains fact-checked, high-yield data from the **Economic Survey of India 2025-26**, presented in Parliament by the Union Finance Minister on **January 29, 2026**.

---

## 📅 Background & Theme

*   **Prepared By:** Economic Division, Department of Economic Affairs, Ministry of Finance.
*   **Chief Economic Adviser (CEA):** Dr. V. Anantha Nageswaran.
*   **Theme:** **"Pushing the Growth Frontier"**
*   **Significance for BPSC:** Tabled ahead of the Union Budget, it assesses the overall health, structural indicators, and sectoral growth rates of the Indian economy.

## 🌾 Sectoral Performance (FY 2025-26)

### 1. Agriculture & Allied Sectors
*   **Sector Growth (Q2):** Registered a growth of **3.5%** in the second quarter of FY26.
*   **5-Year Average Growth:** Average annual growth of **4.4%** at constant prices.
*   **Decadal Performance (FY16–FY25):** The sector registered a decade-high growth rate of **4.45%**.
*   **Key Growth Drivers:** Driven heavily by allied sectors:
    *   **Livestock:** grew at **7.1%** average.
    *   **Fishing & Aquaculture:** grew at **8.8%** average.
    *   **Crops:** grew at **3.5%** average.

### 2. Industrial Sector
*   **Sector Growth (H1):** Recorded a growth of **7.0%** in the first half of FY26.
*   **Manufacturing GVA Growth:** Strong acceleration in GVA:
    *   **Q1 FY26:** **7.72%** GVA growth.
    *   **Q2 FY26:** **9.13%** GVA growth.

### 3. Services Sector
*   **Contribution to GVA:** Remains the dominant pillar, contributing **53.6%** to India's total Gross Value Added (GVA) in the first half of FY26.
*   **Services Exports:** Reached a record high of **USD 387.6 Billion** in FY25, bolstering external resilience.
