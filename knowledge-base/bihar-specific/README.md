# 🏛️ Bihar Specific Study Notes (BPSC CCE)

Bihar-specific topics are highly weighted in the BPSC Prelims, accounting for **20 to 25 questions** annually. This section organizes study materials into four core areas.

---

## 📂 Sub-Subject Structure

We recommend organizing notes under the following subdirectories:

1. **`history/`**
   - *Ancient Bihar*: Rise of Magadha, Maurya & Gupta Dynasties, Vaishali (Lichchhavi Republic), Nalanda & Vikramshila Universities, role of Buddhism & Jainism.
   - *Medieval Bihar*: Bakhtiyar Khalji's invasions, Shershah Suri's reign and administration, Bihar under the Mughal Empire.
   - *Modern Bihar*: 1857 Revolt (Kunwar Singh), Champaran Satyagraha (1917), Bihar Socialist Party, Quit India Movement, JP Movement, and key Bihar freedom fighters (Sri Krishna Singh, Anugrah Narayan Sinha).
2. **`geography/`**
   - *Physiography*: Terai region, Gangetic plains, southern plateau.
   - *Drainage System*: Rivers of Bihar (Ganga, Kosi, Gandak, Bagmati, Son, Punpun, Falgu) - their origins, confluences, and tributaries.
   - *Soils & Climate*: Agro-climatic zones of Bihar, rainfall patterns, flood and drought issues.
   - *Resources & Agriculture*: Agricultural produce, irrigation projects, forests, and mineral distribution (mica, pyrites, coal in small quantities).
3. **`economy-census/`**
   - *Bihar Budget & Economic Survey*: Current year's GDP growth rate, fiscal deficit, sector-wise allocations (especially education, agriculture, health).
   - *Census 2011 (Bihar Specific)*: Literacy rates (male vs. female), sex ratio, child sex ratio, population density (highest in India), decadal growth, district-wise rankings (highest/lowest literacy, population, sex ratio).
4. **`polity/`**
   - *Administration*: Governor, Chief Minister, State Legislature (Bicameral: Vidhan Sabha & Vidhan Parishad), Panchayati Raj (Bihar Panchayati Raj Act, 50% reservation for women), administrative divisions.

---

## 🎯 BPSC Preparation Strategy

- **Rivers & Tributaries Map**: Draw or keep a visual map of north-flowing and south-flowing tributaries of the Ganga. BPSC regularly asks about their confluence points.
- **Census Data Memorization**: Make a 1-page table of the top 3 and bottom 3 districts for literacy, sex ratio, and population density. This yields at least 2-3 direct marks.
- **Champaran & 1857**: Ensure you know the exact names and dates of individuals associated with these events (e.g., Raj Kumar Shukla, peer Ali, Kunwar Singh).
